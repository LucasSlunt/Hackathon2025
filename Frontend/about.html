About Hello, Stars!

Welcome to Hello, Stars!

We are an educational, gamified website designed to teach you all about constellations. Whether you're an astronomy enthusiast or just getting started, our fun and interactive platform will guide you through the stars in the night sky!

Our Mission

At Hello, Stars!, we aim to make learning about constellations fun and engaging. Our goal is to encourage users to explore the night sky, recognize constellations, and engage in friendly competition with their friends to learn together.

Key Features

  • Upload Pictures: Take a picture of the night sky and earn points for identifying constellations.
  • Recognize and Draw Constellations: Test your knowledge by recognizing and drawing constellations to earn more points.
  • Leaderboard: Compete with friends to see who has the most points on our leaderboard!
  • Star Gazing Map: Discover popular spots for star gazing near you.
  • 88 Constellations Database: Learn about all 88 constellations with detailed information and visual guides.

Our Team

Developed by a group of five Computer Science majors for a hackathon, Hello, Stars! was created to bring the beauty of the night sky into an interactive, educational experience. Our team combines a love for both technology and astronomy, and we hope to inspire others to explore the stars.
